# ReplyQuick — AI Google Review Replies on Autopilot

## The Problem (Validated)
- 18M+ Google Business Profiles in the US alone
- 88% of consumers prefer businesses that reply to ALL reviews (BrightLocal 2024)
- 49% more revenue for businesses that respond to reviews (Harvard Business School)
- Yet most small business owners HATE doing it — it's tedious, emotional, and time-consuming
- They get 5-50 reviews/month and each takes 3-5 minutes to craft a good reply
- That's 1-4 hours/month of painful work they'd happily pay to eliminate

## Why THIS Idea Wins
1. **Proven willingness to pay** — ReplyForMe charges $8/mo, Birdeye charges $300+/mo, GoHighLevel agencies resell this for $200-500/mo
2. **Massive market** — 18M+ businesses, even 0.05% = 9,000 customers
3. **Pennies to run** — Each AI reply costs ~$0.001 (GPT-4o-mini)
4. **Dead simple product** — Connect Google → AI replies → Done
5. **Recurring revenue** — Reviews never stop, so neither does the subscription
6. **Low competition at our price point** — Big players (Birdeye, Podium) charge $300+/mo. ReplyForMe is $8/mo but waitlist-only. We slot in at $29/mo with more features.

## Our Edge Over ReplyForMe ($8/mo)
- Dashboard with analytics (they have none — email only)
- Multi-platform (Google + Yelp + Facebook, not just Google)
- Review request SMS/email tool (get MORE reviews, not just reply)
- Sentiment trends and insights
- Better pricing for agencies managing multiple locations

## Revenue Model
- Solo: $29/month (1 location, unlimited replies)
- Growth: $79/month (up to 5 locations + review requests + analytics)
- Agency: $199/month (25 locations + white-label + API)

## Path to $1M/month
- $29 × 10,000 solo customers = $290K
- $79 × 5,000 growth customers = $395K  
- $199 × 1,600 agency customers = $318K
- Total mix: ~$1M/month at ~16,600 customers

## Tech Stack
- Next.js + Tailwind (Vercel free tier)
- Supabase (auth + database)
- OpenAI GPT-4o-mini (reply generation)
- Google Business Profile API (read reviews + post replies)
- Stripe (payments)
- Resend (email notifications)

## Monthly Costs (MVP)
